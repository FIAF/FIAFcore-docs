# Has Sound Characteristic
#### Class
FIAFcore:hasSoundCharacteristic
#### Reference
FIAF Moving Image Cataloguing Manual 2.3.4.3, 3.1.5.3
#### Definition
(2.3.4.3) Sound characteristics are technical specifications relating to the placement of sound on a Manifestation. (3.1.5.3) Technical specifications relating to the fixation of sound in a moving image Manifestation/Item.

#### Domain
[Item](https://fiafcore.org/ontology/Item), [Manifestation](https://fiafcore.org/ontology/Manifestation)
#### Range
[Sound Characteristic](https://fiafcore.org/ontology/SoundCharacteristic)
