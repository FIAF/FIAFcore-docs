# Has Manifestation
#### Class
FIAFcore:hasManifestation
#### Reference
FIAF Moving Image Cataloguing Manual 1.4.6
#### Definition
(1.4.6) Express the relationship between a moving image Work or Variant and a moving image Manifestation (e.g., Part/part of). Describe or demonstrate Work-to-Manifestation relationships through linking to the Work identifier, through the usage of relator terms, or according to the confines of your data structure.

#### Domain
[Variant](https://fiafcore.org/ontology/Variant), [Work](https://fiafcore.org/ontology/Work)
#### Range
[Manifestation](https://fiafcore.org/ontology/Manifestation)
