# Has Variant
#### Class
FIAFcore:hasVariant
#### Reference
FIAF Moving Image Cataloguing Manual 1.4.5
#### Definition
(1.4.5) Express the relationship between a moving image Work and a moving image Variant (e.g., Part/part of). Describe or demonstrate Work-to-Variant relationships through linking to the Work identifier, through the usage of relator terms, or according to the confines of your data structure.
